Taco Recipe

Description

A taco is a traditional Mexican dish of a folded or rolled tortilla that's filled with a variety of ingredients and eaten by hand. Tacos can be made with corn or wheat tortillas and are typically hand-sized. The fillings can include seasoned meat, vegetables, cheese, beans, or seafood, and they can be garnished with condiments like salsa, guacamole, or sour cream.

Ingredients

  • Ground Beef
  • Tortillas
  • Cheese
  • Lettuce

Steps

  1. Cook ground beef on medium high pan
  2. Warm up tortillas
  3. Assemble the tacos
