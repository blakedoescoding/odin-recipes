Pasta Recipe

Description

Pasta aglio e olio is an Italian dish. It’s ultra-easy to make with just four ingredients — garlic (aglio), olive oil (olio), spaghetti, and a hint of red chile peppers

Ingredients

  • Garlic
  • Olive Oil
  • Pasta
  • Red Chili Pepper

Steps

  1. In a medium high heat pan add Oil
  2. Throw in red pepper and garlic until golden
  3. After pasta is done put it in pan with garlic and peppers
  4. Serve.
